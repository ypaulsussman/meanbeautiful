bennett blalock-doane's miniatures and painting

This is the blog & website for Minneapolis-based painter Bennett Blalock-Doane. Most of my work is modeled in 28mm scale, but I have many examples of larger and smaller works.

If you have questions regarding my work or commissions you’d like painted, please feel free to contact me. I also have a Facebook page I never check and can’t be bothered to delete!

Cheers, and enjoy the minis.
